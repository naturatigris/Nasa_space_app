About Us

At Green Minds, we are committed to educating and inspiring individuals to take action against climate change. Our mission is to provide accessible information and resources that empower communities to make informed decisions for a sustainable future.

Key Features

Interactive Data Visualizations for Climate Change with an ARIMA model for predictions.

Customizable Dashboard for in-depth data analysis

Interactive Quiz for educational engagement

Storytelling through Blogs and Podcasts on Climate Science

Multimedia Gallery showcasing climate-related content.

"Global Protector" Game to promote environmental awareness.

Image Slider to visually present future scenarios.
