Introduction to Climate Change

Climate change refers to significant changes in global temperatures and weather patterns over time. While climate change is a natural phenomenon, scientific evidence shows that human activities have been the primary driver of global warming since the mid-20th century.

The increase in greenhouse gases like carbon dioxide (CO2) and methane (CH4) in the atmosphere has led to rising global temperatures, more frequent extreme weather events, and melting ice caps.
